How do we protect your information?

Using encryption to protect your data when it is in transit. Storing your data in secure facilities. Limiting access to your data to authorized personnel. How long do we keep your information? We keep your information for as long as you have an account with us or as needed to provide you with the app and services. We may also keep your information for a longer period of time if we are required to do so by law or if we believe that keeping it is necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others.
